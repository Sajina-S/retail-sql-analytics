# Business Insights from Retail Sales Analysis

## Customer Behavior
- A small group of repeat customers contributes the majority of total revenue.
- Customers with multiple orders have significantly higher lifetime value.
- Some customers have become inactive, indicating opportunities for re-engagement campaigns.

## Sales Trends
- Monthly revenue shows a steady increase over time, suggesting growing customer demand.
- Metro cities generate higher revenue compared to smaller cities.

## Product Performance
- Electronics products contribute the highest revenue.
- Certain products dominate their category, indicating strong customer preference.
- Revenue distribution across products is uneven, highlighting key profit drivers.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus retention efforts on high-value customers.
- Promote best-selling products to maximize revenue.
- Design targeted offers to re-activate inactive customers.
